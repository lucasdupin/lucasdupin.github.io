Nike Running

I feel really proud when projects go beyond advertisement.
This time we took part in a Nike event to help people experience their product's new features and to exercise!

To win this game you need to prentend you're running as fast as you can! Each step will give you 1 point and you'll have 30 seconds.
There was a live ranking where you could check your score and compete with other guys who attended to the event.

We used:

  • OpenFrameworks
  • OpenCV
  • RFID wristbands for ID
  • Depth cameras (Kinect)
  • Big TVs
  • 9 laptops
