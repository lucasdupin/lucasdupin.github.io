Grow With Us

This job might seem simple at first but it's anything but that.
We've build an interactive streamming for showing people how tomatoes are cultivated in Unilever's farm in Chile.

Our structure included:

  • 2 farmers with cameras on top of their hats
  • One camera tracking the sun's position and taking snapshots for a timelapse
  • Spotify integration that lets you choose the currently playing music in the farm.

And that's just the tip of the iceberg, to make this happen we needed to:

  • Write ruby scripts to monitor and take snapshots of the cameras
  • Trigger After Effects rendering through command line
  • Convert videos to web formats using ffmpeg on demand
  • Schedule cron tasks
  • Remotely access and monitor the operation

This is a Black & White video due to agency visual language but I promise you, the site was quite green and red =]
